  • The Army Procurement Fraud Division publishes a newsletter, the "Procurement Fraud Advisor." The newsletter informs the Army's Procurement Fraud Advisors, investigators, and others involved in fighting fraud of new developments, suspension and debarment actions, and other useful information. The most recent update newsletters are publicly accessible and posted below. If you have a JAGCNET account and want to review archived newsletters, just log into JAGCNET and click on our Library link on the left navigation bar.
  • If you are interested in reviewing the names of all individuals suspended or debarred by the Army, you may access the GSA's Excluded Party List directly at GSA's System for Award Management (SAM) at https://www.sam.gov/
